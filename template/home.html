About us

NPS Hospital is a Registered Charity under the Charities Acts (Registered Charity No. 20000394) and is administered and managed in accordance with a Hospital Constitution approved by the Charities Regulatory Authority. The current Hospital Constitution was approved in 1918. The property is vested in Trustees. NPS Hospital is a health care institution providing patient treatment with specialized medical and nursing staff and medical equipment.

“Our Mission is to bring healthcare of International standards within the reach of every individual. We are committed to the achievement and maintenance of excellence in education, research and healthcare for the benefit of humanity”
